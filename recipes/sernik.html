Sernik

Description

Polish cheesecake

Ingredience

  • 200g Creem Cheese
  • 200ml Yogurt
  • 200g Sugar
  • Froots
  • 4 Eggs
  • 3Tbsp Cornflour
  • 1 tsp Vanila

Method:

  1. Separate and whip egg whites
  2. Mix cream chees, yogurt, sugar, cornflour and vanilla in a separate bowl
  3. fold in egg whites
  4. Line baking tin and fill with mix
  5. Top with froot
  6. Bake 1hr at 180
